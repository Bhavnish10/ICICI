Key Risk Factors

Economic Slowdown

Sharp growth deceleration could dent loan volumes and elevate NPA provisions, particularly in unsecured retail and SME portfolios.

Asset Quality Shocks

RBI audit caused one-off provision spike. Large corporate defaults could pressure GNPA ratios above current lows.

Regulatory Changes

Higher capital requirements, revised priority-sector norms, or fee caps could constrain loan growth or compress fee income.

Interest Rate Volatility

Rapid rate cuts could compress NIM before loan book repricing; rapid hikes could stress unsecured borrowers.

Competitive Pressure

Fintech entrants and aggressive private peers compete in cards, personal loans, and SME lending, potentially pressuring yields.
